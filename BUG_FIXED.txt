What Was Wrong
========================================

  to call .config() on a StringVar object, but only
  Label widgets have the .config() method.

Location:
  Line 356, 359 in book_translator_gui.pyw
  Method: update_api_status()

Error message:
  AttributeError: 'StringVar' object has no
  attribute 'config'

Before (WRONG):
